Now as a Senior Property Manager, Matthew exhibits exceptional customer assistance and strong leadership skills. He is licensed in the State of Illinois as a Community Association Manager. Matthew continues to gain more knowledge in his profession and has recently received his CPM (Certified Property Manager) designation through IREM. Matthew also received his AMS (Association Management Specialist) certification and is working towards his PCAM, the highest accreditation in property management.
